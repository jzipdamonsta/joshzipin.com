Adventurer

My parents tell me that at 9 months I skipped crawling and went straight to running. My mom has been beating herself up about it for years but I think I turned out fine.

I played Division I soccer at Davidson College and represented the United States in the Maccabiah Games in Israel.

I was friends with Stephen Curry in college. He crushed me in golf once.

I went to the World Cup in Brazil in 2014 and saw this in person.

I like to run far. I once ran rim to rim in the Grand Canyon. Another time I was a part of a relay from San Francisco to LA. I also did the 5 peaks challenge with my friend Gil.

For three years I led the SF chapter of a free fitness movement called November Project. There’s probably one near you. #justshowup

I have an amazing group of friends. In 2017 we rode our bikes from San Francisco to Anchorage, Alaska. Clif Bar made a short film about it.

In 2018 we rode from Vancouver, BC to Halifax, Nova Scotia–clear across Canada. No one filmed it, but we raised $5k to clean up our oceans. Strava posted this photo of me.

I once organized an attempt to break the world record for most people doing a handstand. We had enough people but Guinness rejected us on some technicality. Their loss, really.

I’ve climbed Mount Shasta (14,180 feet) twice. The second time we cracked coconuts at the top.

I love grand adventures with great people. If we should go on an adventure together, reach out.
